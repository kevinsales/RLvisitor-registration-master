Please submit this form at least 2 days before your entry date to give ample time for the approval process. Submission of this form does not automatically guarantee entry. Please wait for notification of approval via email before attempting to enter the campus.

This request form may be used by alumni, partner libraries, vendors/suppliers, non-enrolled students, and researchers who want to enter the Rizal Library for different purposes. Alumni and partner libraries may also use this form to borrow/return books and to avail of RL's digitization service.
